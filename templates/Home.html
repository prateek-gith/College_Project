About Us

Welcome to SEP Doorstep Delight

SEP Doorstep Delight was founded in 2018 by SEP Group. The Group was created by 3 friends, Mr. Prateek Yadav, Mohd. Sameer Saifi and Ms. Erum Nawaz who later founded SEP Doorstep Delight.

Today in this hectic life, its hard for the people living alone for the studies, for work or the old people to have a proper and healthy meal whole day.For such people we have introduced this platform where the person can be our member and select the plan accordingly, even they can customize the particular menu with other option available by informing us one day prior.
